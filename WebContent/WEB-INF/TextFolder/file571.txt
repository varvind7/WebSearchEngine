For years he's been bringing together artists from around the world to play in the Silk Road Ensemble - creating an international dialogue with instruments. Their journeys are now being told in a new documentary called The Music of Strangers. Jane O'Brien caught up with Yo-Yo Ma and some ensemble members in Washington DC, for a special performance.
