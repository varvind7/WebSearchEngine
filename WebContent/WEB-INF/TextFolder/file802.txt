In an exclusive interview with the BBC, Senior General Min Aung Hlaing said he believe the election would be both free and fair, but that the army's role in politics would continue. It is expected that the party of Nobel Peace Prize winner Aung San Suu Kyi will win the most seats, but the constitution bars her from becoming president.
