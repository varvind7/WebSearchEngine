Brazil's Public Prosecutor's office has launched an inquiry into whether a new Facebook app "offends the honour" of Brazilian men. The Lulu app allows female users to rate publicly the men they know. It has 3 million users worldwide, and has clocked up half a billion profile views, according to Lulu. It has led to a national debate in Brazil about sex, decency and privacy.
