SteadXP bolts onto most models of camera and then tracks their movements in order to stabilise the video they film and deliver movie-style footage. The project had raised more than €500,000 ($529,600; £429,800) on Kickstarter and had originally targeted a March 2016 release. Chris Foxx reports from Las Vegas.
