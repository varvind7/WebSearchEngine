Self/Less is a new sci-fi thriller opening at the box office this weekend which presents a hi-tech world where the privileged can have their consciousness transferred into the body of a younger person so they can live forever. The picture stars Ryan Reynolds and Sir Ben Kingsley. Several critics have claimed the film bears a likeness to a 1966 US sci-fi drama called Seconds.
