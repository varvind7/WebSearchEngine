The algorithm uses a variety of factors such as the actors involved, genre and what the film is about and combines them into a massive data set. The algorithm uses the data to then determine whether a film will be profitable. BBC Click spoke to Dr Kang Zhao about the potential for such a technology and what films the algorithm predicted might do well this year.
