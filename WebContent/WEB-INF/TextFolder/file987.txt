Donald Trump's plans to build a wall along the US border with Mexico has drawn much attention during the course of a sometimes-turbulent campaign season. But across the country's other border, many Canadians worry about how their lives would be affected with a President Trump in charge of their big neighbour to the south. Jane O'Brien travelled to Montreal to learn more. Produced by Mat Morrison. Filmed by Ian Druce.
