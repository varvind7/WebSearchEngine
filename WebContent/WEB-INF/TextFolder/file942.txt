There's even a board game. Now the Bard has entered the realm of virtual reality with an immersive video game called Play the Knave, developed by the University of California, Davis. Jane O'Brien went to the Mondavi Center for the Performing Arts near Sacramento to see it in action. Edited by Bill McKenna.
